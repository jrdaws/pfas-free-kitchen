## Product Accuracy Guidelines

All Wave 13 product prompts require:

1. **Real Products Only** - No fabricated products
2. **Accurate Specifications** - Verified from manufacturer
3. **Conservative Verification** - No Tier 3/4 without actual lab reports
4. **Proper Categorization** - Correct category and material assignments
5. **Valid Retailer Links** - Real ASINs and product URLs where possible

---

## Data Sources

Products should be researched from:
- Manufacturer websites (all-clad.com, lodgecastiron.com, etc.)
- Retailer listings (Amazon, Williams Sonoma, etc.)
- Industry publications
- Material safety data sheets

Health/educational content should cite:
- EPA (epa.gov/pfas)
- CDC/ATSDR
- Peer-reviewed publications
- FTC guidance documents

## Anti-Patterns (DO NOT USE)

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use Instead |
|----------|----------------|
| `text-blue-*` | `text-orange-*` or `text-primary` |
| `bg-purple-*` | `bg-orange-*` or `bg-primary` |
| `bg-indigo-*` | `bg-stone-*` or `bg-surface` |
| `border-blue-*` | `border-orange-*` or `border-primary` |
| `ring-blue-*` | `ring-orange-*` |
| `shadow-blue-*` | `shadow-orange-*` |

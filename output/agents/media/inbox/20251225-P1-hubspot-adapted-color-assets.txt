## Design Principles to Follow

1. **Orange is the star** - Use for CTAs, links, and primary actions only
2. **Navy adds depth** - Use for secondary buttons, headers, footer
3. **White backgrounds** - Clean, professional, content-focused
4. **Green only for success** - Checkmarks, success messages, ready badges
5. **No competing accents** - Remove teal, purple, or other attention-grabbing colors

## Review Checklist

### Color Harmony
- [ ] Orange and Navy don't clash (complementary warm/cool balance)
- [ ] Primary orange is the star (most prominent for CTAs)
- [ ] Navy adds depth without competing
- [ ] No purple, teal, or other rogue accent colors

### Contrast (WCAG AA)
- [ ] Text on backgrounds: 4.5:1 minimum
- [ ] Navy/White: ~10.5:1 ✓
- [ ] Terminal text: 4.9:1 ✓
- [ ] Orange buttons: 3.4:1 (acceptable for large text/buttons)

### Component Review
- [ ] Buttons are clearly distinguishable (primary vs secondary)
- [ ] Cards have appropriate hover states
- [ ] Terminal is readable
- [ ] Footer with navy looks professional
- [ ] Success/warning/error colors still work semantically

### Overall
- [ ] Feels professional and premium
- [ ] Orange pops without being overwhelming
- [ ] Clean and content-focused
- [ ] Works for developer audience

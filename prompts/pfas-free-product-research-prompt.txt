# PFAS-FREE KITCHEN PRODUCT RESEARCH PROMPT

## Mission

You are a product safety researcher specializing in PFAS (per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances) contamination in kitchen products. Your task is to systematically identify, verify, and catalog PFAS-free kitchen products across all categories using a rigorous evidence-based methodology.

---

## VERIFICATION METHODOLOGY (Mandatory)

For EVERY product identified, you MUST document verification status for each criterion:

| Code | Verification Method | How to Verify |
|------|---------------------|---------------|
| LAB | Lab Test Results Available | Search for "[Brand] PFAS lab test results" or "[Brand] third party testing" |
| CERT | Third-Party Certification | Look for SGS, Intertek, NSF, MADE SAFE, OEKO-TEX, ISO 17025 certifications |
| IND | Independent Testing | Search Mamavation, Consumer Reports, Ecology Center, LeafScore |
| BRAND | Brand Testing Confirmed | Check brand website for testing claims, AB1200/Prop 65 disclosures |
| SUPPLY | Supply Chain Verified | Check manufacturing location, material sourcing transparency |
| MAT | Inherently Safe Material | Cast iron, stainless steel, carbon steel, glass, silicone = auto-qualify |

**Scoring Formula:**
- LAB = +2, CERT = +2, IND = +2, BRAND = +1, SUPPLY = +1, MAT = +2
- Maximum Score: 10/10
- **ONLY include products scoring 6/10 or higher**

## RESEARCH PROCESS (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Identify Products
For each category, search:
```
site:amazon.com [Brand] [Product Type] ASIN
```
Record: Product name, Brand, ASIN, Price

### Step 2: Verify PFAS Status
For each product, search in order:

**A. Check Legal Disclosures (Required for CA/CO sales)**
```
site:[brand].com AB1200 chemical disclosure
site:[brand].com california prop 65 cookware
```

**B. Check Independent Testing**
```
site:mamavation.com [Brand] [Product]
site:consumerreports.org [Brand] PFAS
site:leafscore.com [Brand] review
site:ecocenter.org [Brand]
```

**C. Check Brand Testing Claims**
```
[Brand] PFAS free lab test
[Brand] third party testing SGS Intertek
[Brand] Thermolon certification (for GreenPan)
```

**D. Check for Red Flags**
```
[Brand] [Product] PFAS lawsuit
[Brand] [Product] Prop 65 warning
[Brand] cookware PTFE FEP PFA disclosure
```

### Step 3: Assign Score
| Criteria Met | Score |
|--------------|-------|
| LAB + CERT + IND + BRAND + SUPPLY + MAT | 10/10 |
| CERT + IND + BRAND + SUPPLY + MAT | 9/10 |
| LAB + CERT + IND + BRAND | 8/10 |
| CERT + BRAND + SUPPLY + MAT | 7/10 |
| CERT + BRAND + MAT | 6/10 |
| Below 6 | DO NOT INCLUDE |

### Step 4: Document Evidence
For each product, record:
- Verification sources (URLs)
- Date verified
- Any caveats or warnings

## CRITICAL WARNINGS

### Products to EXCLUDE (Confirmed PFAS)

**Per California AB1200 Disclosures:**
- Le Creuset nonstick lines (PTFE, FEP, PFA)
- All-Clad nonstick lines (PTFE)
- Instant Pot (PTFE, PFA, FEP on cooking surfaces)
- Calphalon nonstick

**Per Mamavation Testing:**
- COSORI air fryers (all models)
- Ninja air fryers (most models)
- Cuisinart air fryers
- Hamilton Beach air fryers (except PFAS-Free model)
- Bella/Beautiful air fryers
- Dash air fryers

### Products Requiring Extra Verification
- Any product claiming "PFOA-free" (may still contain other PFAS)
- Products with "ceramic coating" (verify brand tests for all PFAS, not just PFOA)
- Silicone products (verify platinum-grade, not food-grade)
